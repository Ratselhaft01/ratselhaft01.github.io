Where We Came From

Our beginning was very humble. We were just college students with dreams of changing the world! Of course many college students dream of changing the world, but we decided to press forward anyway. We learned, developed, and eventually worked on some small projects. Eventually we graduated and started working for different companies. We stayed in touch, however, and continued to work on our projects in our free time. Eventually we got some attention and even a few small grants. Our projects have expanded and now we can finally fulfill those college dreams. With the donations that we recieve, we are able to get better equipment and spend more of our time on these projects.
